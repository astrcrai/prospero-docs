Documentation for the Prospero HPC facility
==========================================================

**Prospero** is the centralised high performance computing (HPC) facility for research at `Liverpool John Moores University <https://ljmu.ac.uk/>`_. The facility is hosted and administered by LJMU's `IT services division <https://www.ljmu.ac.uk/itservices>`_.

.. image:: images/hpe_apollo2000.png
  :width: 800
  :alt: Prospero

Prospero is available for use by all research staff and postgraduate students at LJMU, subject to usage policies.
